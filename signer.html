How to Sign Your PDF – 4 Quick Steps

  1. Upload Your PDF: Drag and drop or click "Browse PDF File" to open any contract, form, or document.

  2. Create Your Signature: Click "Signature", "Initials", or "Stamp" in the toolbar. Draw, type, or upload as you prefer.

  3. Place It on the PDF: Click anywhere on the page to place your signature. Drag it to the exact position, resize from the corner.

  4. Download: Click the green Download button. Your signature is permanently embedded and the PDF is saved to your device.
