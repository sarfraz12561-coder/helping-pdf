How to Sign Your PDF – 4 Simple Steps

  1. Upload Your PDF: Drag and drop or click "Browse PDF File" to open your contract, form, or document.

  2. Create Your Signature: Click "Add Signature" — draw it with mouse or touch, type your name in a beautiful font, add initials, or upload a signature/stamp image.

  3. Place It on the PDF: Click anywhere on the page to place your signature. Drag to reposition, pull the corner to resize. Add it on multiple pages if needed.

  4. Download: Click the green Download button. Your signature is permanently embedded in the PDF and saved directly to your device.

🔒 Is My Document & Signature Safe?

Completely. Your PDF and signature data never leave your device. No file is uploaded to any external server. Everything runs inside your browser using trusted open-source libraries — PDF.js and PDF-lib. Once the page loads, it works fully offline.
