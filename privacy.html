Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Welcome to Helping PDF. One of our main priorities is the privacy and security of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by Helping PDF and how we use it.

1. Local Processing (100% Secure)

Unlike standard online PDF utilities, Helping PDF processes your documents directly inside your web browser using client-side WebAssembly and JavaScript technology. Your files are never uploaded to our servers, never stored, and never shared with any third party. All processing stays strictly on your personal device.

2. No Account Required

We operate under a strict "No Sign-up, No Email" policy. You are not required to create an account, provide your email address, or enter any personal identification data to use any of our tools. You can start using our features instantly.

3. Log Files & Analytics

Helping PDF follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. The information collected by log files includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamp, referring/exit pages, and possibly the number of clicks. These are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable. The purpose of the information is for analyzing trends and administering the site.

4. Google AdSense & Cookies

Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to your website or other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to your users based on their visit to your sites and/or other sites on the Internet. Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Ads Settings.

5. Consent

By using our website, you hereby consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.
